WHAT YOU CAN DO

Free Exploration

Free exploration is the best way to get a quick visual overview on your collection. Wanna know which games you have played the most in recent times? Or how many of your games can fit 6+ players at the table? What about the overall complexity of your games? These are the trends that you can easily spot on Free Exploration .

Automated Insights

If you just want to know interesting facts and patterns about your collection and how you compare with the rest of the community, Automated Insights is the best way to go about it. Automated insights are generated for you based on your collection items, play and rating history on BGG.

Community Stats & Trends

It is often said that we are living the Golden Age of Boardgames due to the boom in popularity that the industry has experienced in recent years. This section aims to shed light on this topic by looking at global data trends that have shaped the community since its conception to this day.
